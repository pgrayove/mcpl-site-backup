A guideline is a custom selection of Accessibility Tests to run. The Accessible Content module comes with the following guidelines:

  • Section 508
  • WCAG 1.0 (A)
  • WCAG 1.0 (AA)
  • WCAG 1.0 (AAA)
  • WCAG 2.0 (A)
  • WCAG 2.0 (AA)
  • WCAG 2.0 (AAA)

Guidelines are regular nodes, you can create a new guideline at node/add/accessibility-guideline and check off the tests that apply to your guideline by looking at the tests checkboxes under the field set Accessibility Tests.
